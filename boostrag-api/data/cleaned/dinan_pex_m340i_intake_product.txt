Summary:
Dinan offers a PEX cold air intake for G2x B58 Mx40i models. The page emphasizes increased airflow, improved throttle response, louder induction sound, and a larger air box and filter area than stock.

Specifications and Features:
- Claimed max gains of 9 WHP and 8 lb-ft of torque on a stock vehicle
- High heat-deflection PEX construction with OEM-style appearance
- Louder, sportier sound
- Improved throttle response
- Claimed to outflow factory intake by 58% or more
- Larger air box (60% larger than stock)
- 40% increase in filter area (stock: 82 in², Dinan: 115 in²)
- Compatible with all G2x Mx40i models with and without factory bracing
- Quick and easy installation

Fitment:
- ECS fitment list includes BMW G20 M340i and G20 M340i xDrive among supported vehicles

Notes:
- Not legal for sale or use in California on pollution-controlled vehicles, according to the page
- ECS page indicates Lifetime Replacement Program eligibility
