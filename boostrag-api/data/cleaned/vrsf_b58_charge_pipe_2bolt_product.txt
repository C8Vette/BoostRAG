- Includes one plugged 1/8 NPT bung
- NPT bung can be used for water injection, boost leak testing, or as a boost source
- Includes 4-ply silicone couplers
- Includes stainless steel T-bolt clamps
- Page states the OEM charge pipe is prone to failure at stock and tuned boost levels
